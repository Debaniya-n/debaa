About Macchako Lounge

Green as a Tea Leaf

The phillosophy behind the macchajins

Have you ever heard the phrase that a person is still green?
that's what we are, New to the world of art,
having big dreams of becoming a good artist just like those who have inspired us and many others.

As a group of collective individuals, we all have our own unique traits in ourselves, yet with such diversities, we're still dedicated to achieving our goal united as a team.

All these aspects are what have described us.
Therefore we use maccha to symbolize ourselves.
The green which is its primary color symbolizes
that we're still learning on our path.
and all of the possible maccha brews that vary between each preference symbolize our diversities.
by debaa_nyan
