Guide to Commissioning the ba!


Before proceeding please make sure that i am available for commissions, you could do that by messaging me at twitter @debaa_nyan or my discord @"Debaa"#8969. Keep in mind that Lewd Commissions would cost you an extra 30%

If I happen to have an ongoing project, Your requests would be put on queue until I'm available to work on it. Otherwise I will usually send the initial draft(s) in the first few hours.

Average working time is around 3 to 10 days After the initial sketch (excluding Weekends). During that time period, please refrain from cancelling your orders.

During the working period, you are eligible to request up to 2 revisions. After that, there will be a USD20 fee for each revisions

Payments could be done Anytime Prior or During the working period whether it's a Full Payment or in Stages. Though it's recommended to do so After the initial draft, Preferably with a Full Payment.

Payments are done either through My Ko-fi, Streamlabs, or Preferrably PayPal. Though, i could also accept Something else if negotiated. ;9
