Terms and Conditions

Guide to Commissioning the ba!


Before proceeding please make sure that I am available for commissions, you could do that by messaging me at Twitter @debaa_nyan or my Discord @"Debaa"#8969.

Also keep in mind that for Lewd Commissions, client must state that their characters are at least 18 of age.

If I happen to have an ongoing project, Your requests would be put on hold until I'm available to work on it. Otherwise I will usually send the initial draft(s) in the first few hours.

Average working time is around 10 to 45 days after the initial sketch (excluding Weekends). During that time period, please refrain from cancelling your orders.

During the working period, you are eligible to request up to 2 revisions. After that, there will be a USD20 fee for each revisions

Payments could be done anytime prior or during the working period whether it's a full payment or in stages. Though it's recommended to do so After the initial draft, Preferably with a full payment.

Payments are done either through my Ko-fi, Streamlabs, or preferrably PayPal. Though, I could also accept something else if negotiated. ;9
